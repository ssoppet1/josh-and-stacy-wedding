Booking Rooms

To book your room at Sandos Finisterra Los Cabos, please contact Maureen Grapenthin from PS Travel Inc by calling (708) 533-2343 or by emailing her at msgrapenthin@yahoo.com.

If you are able to attend our wedding, please try to have your room booked through Maureen by October 1 at the latest as the rooms for our wedding are only being held for a limited period of time.

We have held 15 rooms, a mix of Junior Suite Ocean View rooms and regular Junior Suites. The dates are from January 14, 2024 through January 21, 2024. You can book for any amount of nights within that time frame. Rates vary based on the amount of nights and how many people will be sharing the room.

If you have points or miles and wish to book your own air, please send Maureen the flight information for transfers or let her know if you would prefer for her to book the air for you.

The final balance for your room will be due October 15, 2023.

When you reach out to Maureen to book, she will also be asking if you wish to book trip protection. There are two kinds: credit back or cash back. They are both different prices, which she will go over with you. When you book, you will pay for the trip protection and a deposit. Then you can either pay after booking or pay the total balance by October 15, 2023.

We can't wait to see you in Mexico!
